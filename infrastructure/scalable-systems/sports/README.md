# Sports Infrastructure & Governance Framework

## Overview

The Sports Infrastructure & Governance Framework establishes ScrollVerse as an innovative force in sports management, athlete development, fan engagement, and community athletics. This framework combines AI-powered analytics, blockchain-based governance, and community ownership models to revolutionize how sports organizations operate.

## Core Components

### 1. Professional Sports Franchise Management

#### Ownership & Governance Model

**Hybrid Ownership Structure**:
- **Sovereign Control**: Chais Hill maintains majority control and strategic direction
- **Community Ownership**: NFT-based fractional ownership for fans
- **Player Equity**: Performance-based ownership stakes for key players
- **Local Investment**: Partnership opportunities for local businesses and investors

**DAO Governance Features**:
- Fan voting on non-critical decisions (uniform designs, community initiatives)
- Transparent financial reporting via blockchain
- Community proposals for team improvements
- Token-weighted voting system
