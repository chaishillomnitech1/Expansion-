# Nevada Music & Sports Centers of Excellence (CoE)

## Overview
The Nevada Centers of Excellence represent Omnitech1's flagship integrated facilities combining music production, sports training, and blockchain technology.

## Location
**Las Vegas, Nevada**
- Primary Hub: Las Vegas Strip District
- Secondary Facility: Henderson Innovation Center

## Facilities

### Music Production Center
- **Recording Studios**: 5 world-class recording studios
- **Production Suites**: 10 digital audio workstations
- **Live Performance Space**: 500-seat venue
- **Equipment**: Industry-leading audio equipment and instruments
- **Technology Integration**: Blockchain-based royalty tracking via ScrollCoin

### Sports Training Center
- **Athletic Facilities**: Basketball, volleyball, and multi-purpose courts
- **Training Areas**: Weight room, cardio center, recovery suites
- **Technology**: Performance analytics and biometric tracking
- **Capacity**: 200+ athletes simultaneously

### Blockchain Integration Hub
- **Smart Contract Management**: Automated royalty distribution
- **NFT Minting Station**: Music and sports memorabilia tokenization
- **ScrollCoin Treasury**: On-site token management and distribution
- **LayerZero Bridge**: Omnichain asset transfer capabilities

## Features

### Music & Entertainment
- Professional recording and mastering services
- Artist development programs
- Live streaming capabilities
- Music NFT marketplace integration

### Sports & Athletics
- Professional coaching and training programs
- Youth development leagues
- Performance analytics and data tracking
- Sports memorabilia NFT programs

### Technology & Innovation
- Web3 authentication and access control
- Blockchain-based ticketing system
- Real-time royalty distribution via ScrollCoin

## Integration with ScrollCoin

### Token Utility
- **Facility Access**: Members pay with ScrollCoin tokens
- **Royalty Payments**: Artists receive royalties in SCROLL
- **Staking Rewards**: Token holders earn rewards from facility revenue
- **Governance**: Token-based voting on facility improvements
