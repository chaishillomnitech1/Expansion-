## Best Practices

### Document Management
1. Update documents immediately when changes occur
2. Keep both digital and physical backups
3. Use version control for important documents
4. Regular reviews to ensure accuracy
5. Secure storage for sensitive information

### Financial Management
1. Separate business and personal finances
2. Track all income and expenses
3. Reconcile accounts monthly
4. Plan for taxes quarterly
5. Maintain emergency fund

### IP Protection
1. Document creation dates
2. Register important IP promptly
3. Monitor for infringement
4. Use proper notices (©, ™)
5. Maintain evidence of use

### Asset Management
1. Regular inventory updates
2. Adequate insurance coverage
3. Secure storage practices
4. Document all transactions
5. Professional appraisals when needed
