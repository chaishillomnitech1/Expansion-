## Best Practices

### Engagement Tips

1. **Start with energy**: Open with enthusiasm and clear value proposition
2. **Use live data frequently**: Reference OmniMap every 3-5 minutes
3. **Encourage participation**: Ask questions, run polls, acknowledge chat
4. **Tell stories**: Use real examples from network participants
5. **Maintain pacing**: Change visual focus every 60-90 seconds
6. **Create moments**: Plan special reveals or announcements
7. **End with action**: Give clear next steps for participants

### Technical Tips

1. **Bandwidth**: Always have backup internet connection
2. **Rendering**: Use dedicated GPU for OmniMap visualizations
3. **Audio**: Invest in quality microphone (most important)
4. **Lighting**: Three-point lighting minimum for video
5. **Backup**: Record locally even when streaming
6. **Testing**: Go live 15 minutes early for tech check
7. **Monitoring**: Have assistant watching metrics and chat

### Content Tips

1. **Focus**: One main topic per broadcast
2. **Length**: 30-60 minutes optimal (attention span)
3. **Interaction**: 30% teaching, 30% demonstration, 40% interaction
4. **Preparation**: Over-prepare, then improvise naturally
5. **Authenticity**: Be yourself, show personality
6. **Value**: Always deliver actionable insights
7. **Accessibility**: Accommodate different learning styles
